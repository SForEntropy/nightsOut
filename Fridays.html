Fridays

Friday night plays host to Face Down, The Calling and VooDoo.


Face Down

Face Down in a monthly event at Scala night club. It has two rooms which play pop-punk, emo and metal.


The Calling

This is the longest running goth club night in the UK. It is held on the first Friday of every month at the Blue Moon.


VooDoo

This is a monthly event held at Elektrowerks. It has no dress code but asks patrons to 'express themselves'.
